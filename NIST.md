**Excel Spreadsheet:** `NIST-CMMC-Artifacts-by-Level.xlsx` — Complete spreadsheet with 6 sheets (Overview, Domain Comparison, Level 1/2 Artifacts, Critical Documents, Implementation Roadmap)

### Spreadsheet Structure (16 Sheets)

**Main Control Tracking:**
- **Controls+AO** (321 rows) — Full control inventory with Assessment Objectives, NIST 171 IDs, implementation status, POC, artifacts
- **L1 Controls+AO** (59 rows) — Level 1 CMMC controls only
- **171 Self Assess (DFARS)** (112 rows) — NIST 800-171 self-assessment with status tracking

**Management & Tracking:**
- **CRM** (320 rows) — Control Responsibility Matrix (ESP vs OSC responsibilities)
- **POAM** — Plan of Action and Milestones for gaps
- **OPA** — Other Planned Actions
- **Dashboard** — Implementation metrics and compliance status
- **Projects** — Project-based remediation tracking
- **Internal Actions** — Internal compliance actions
- **Periodic Items** — Recurring compliance tasks (e.g., quarterly risk assessments)
- **MSP Actions** — Managed Service Provider actions

**Documentation:**
- **Doc Inventory** — SSP, policies, procedures tracking
- **Assessor Qstns** — Common assessor questions and preparation

**Reference:**
- **Read Me** — Instructions and guidance
- **External Connections** — Third-party systems tracking (FedRAMP, CMMC certification status)

### Key Domains Covered
- Access Control (AC)
- Awareness & Training (AT)
- Incident Response (IR)
- System & Communications Protection (SC)
- System & Information Integrity (SI)
- Maintenance (MA)
- Protection of CUI (PE)
- Risk Assessment (RA)
- Security Assessment (CA)
- Access Control (AC)

### Status Fields
- **Met?** — Implementation status
- **Projected Imp Date** — Target completion
- **POC** — Point of Contact
- **Description of Implementation** — Control implementation details
- **Artifact(s) / Objective Evidence (OE)** — Evidence/documentation
- **Description of Gap(s)** — Identified gaps requiring remediation
