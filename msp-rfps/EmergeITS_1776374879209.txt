Executive summary
Millar Industries is seeking a long-term IT partner that goes beyond reactive support—one that
operates as an extension of internal leadership, proactively manages risk, communicates clearly with
non-technical stakeholders, and maintains a stable, dependable technology environment. Emerge
proposes OmniWATCH Pro as a comprehensive, standards-driven managed services program
designed to prioritize network stability, reduce outage risk, and strengthen cybersecurity while
providing clear operational visibility and accountability.
Millar Industries operates a hybrid on-premises and cloud environment supporting business
operations across multiple locations and teams. With no full-time internal IT staff, predictable
performance, fast response, and disciplined execution are mission-critical. OmniWATCH Pro is built
for organizations that require consistent day-to-day operations and proactive risk reduction—without
the overhead of building and staffing an internal IT department.
OmniWATCH Pro delivers a unified operating model that combines end-user support, infrastructure
management, cybersecurity, and Microsoft 365 administration under a single accountable framework.
Support is provided through a tiered service desk model with defined SLAs, live-answer intake, and
clear escalation paths to senior engineering resources—helping restore service quickly and prevent
repeat incidents that lead to recurring outages.
The primary operational driver of OmniWATCH Pro is network stability and outage prevention.
Endpoints, servers, network devices, and cloud services are continuously monitored, patched, and
maintained against defined standards to reduce unplanned downtime and service disruption.
Cybersecurity controls—including managed antivirus/EDR, MFA enforcement, dark web monitoring,

operations to reduce exposure while improving response readiness.
Emerge also recognizes that Millar Industries leadership requires clear, business-level
communication, not technical noise. OmniWATCH Pro includes structured reporting, shared
dashboards, and a vCIO-style engagement model that connects operational data to business
outcomes—especially stability, continuity, and risk reduction. This governance cadence supports

A. End-user & Helpdesk Support
OmniWATCH Pro includes standard helpdesk support aligned to Millar Industries defined business

Helpdesk Model
The Emerge service desk is built to reduce friction for end users and accelerate resolution through a
human-first, live answer approach. When a user reaches out by phone, our goal is to have a live
person respond quickly, capture the issue clearly, and begin troubleshooting immediately rather than
routing callers into long automated menus or delaying engagement. This approach improves first
contact resolution, strengthens user confidence, and ensures priority issues are identified and acted
upon rapidly.
•

Dispatch (Intake & Triage): First point of contact for phone/email submissions. Dispatch
validates user details, gathers symptoms and impact, assigns priority, and routes the request
to the right resource.

•

Tier 1 Helpdesk (Rapid Resolution): Handles common end-user issues such as password and
account support, Microsoft 365 application assistance, basic device troubleshooting,
printing/peripherals, and standard access requests. Tier 1 focuses on fast restoration of
productivity and clear communication.

•

Tier 2 Helpdesk (Advanced Troubleshooting): Engages when issues require deeper
investigation, configuration changes, cross-system troubleshooting, or more specialized
knowledge (e.g., complex Microsoft 365 issues, endpoint management interactions, recurring
problems, or multi-user impact). Tier 2 may coordinate with vendors or customer IT contacts
as needed.

•

Escalation to TechOps (Technical Operations, including Tier 3 Engineering): TechOps is the
escalation point for complex or high impact reactive support that requires environment level
analysis, stabilization, and advanced engineering/root cause remediation. TechOps also leads
Emerge’s proactive management programs—including enforcement of configuration
standards, monitoring and alert response, configuration drift management, and ongoing
operational improvements.

This tiered model ensures tickets are handled by the right level of expertise as quickly as possible,
while maintaining a clear and accountable escalation path for urgent or complex issues. It also
ensures that reactive support and proactive management remain connected—so recurring issues,

security risks, and operational gaps identified through the helpdesk translate into measurable
improvements through TechOps led operational programs.

C. Cybersecurity Services
OmniWATCH Pro offers a structured cybersecurity program that reduces business risk through
consistent controls, visibility, and response readiness. The program meets cyber insurance
underwriting standards, helping minimize exposures and improve insurer relationships. It follows the
NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF), ensuring a comprehensive approach to risk management by
consistently applying its core functions: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover. This
supports governance, endpoint protection, identity management, and user education, enabling
proactive threat management, industry compliance, and strong controls for stakeholders. The NIST
CSF drives continuous improvement and benchmarking of your security posture.
OmniWATCH Pro is structured to support organizations operating within the Defense Industrial Base
(DIB) by aligning day-to-day Enterprise IT operations with CMMC Level 1 requirements. Under this
engagement, Emerge assumes responsibility for assisting in the implementation, monitoring, and
documentation of applicable Level 1 controls across the Enterprise environment, including identity
management, endpoint protection, patching, logging, backup, and incident response coordination.
CMMC Level 2 requirements are explicitly out of scope for OmniWATCH Pro and are expected to be
met through the DCG-provided enclave. Emerge will collaborate closely with DCG to ensure clear
responsibility boundaries, coordinated remediation where Enterprise dependencies exist, and
audit-ready documentation that supports DCG’s enclave activities without duplication or conflict.
Managed Antivirus (next-gen AV/EDR)
OmniWATCH Pro establishes both traditional antivirus and Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR)
through a single, unified endpoint security solution. This approach pairs signature-based prevention
with behavioral detection and active response capabilities—providing broader protection, faster
containment, and simpler management than running separate AV and EDR tools. The solution
includes 24x7x365 monitoring and response via a third-party SOC provider.

Managed Detection & Response (MDR)
Managed Detection & Response (MDR) is available as an optional add-on to OmniWATCH Pro for
organizations that want 24x7 security operations support beyond endpoint protection. For many
environments, the EDR capabilities included with OmniWATCH Pro provide sufficient detection and
response coverage. When MDR is desired, Emerge scopes and tunes the service to the customer’s
specific environment (users, endpoints, network, identity, cloud footprint, and business workflows).
This requires deeper discovery than an RFP typically allows so we can right-size the solution—
ensuring appropriate investment without over-spending.
Multifactor Authentication (MFA)
Emerge will enforce multifactor authentication (MFA) across key systems and applications with a
guided rollout for users and executives, leveraging the MFA capabilities included in Microsoft 365 as
the default baseline. Emerge will provide ongoing MFA compliance monitoring and reporting
(including enabled/disabled status and policy adherence) and tune policies to reduce unnecessary
prompts while maintaining strong security. Where requirements justify additional investment (for
example, specific application compatibility or advanced MFA workflows), Emerge will validate the
need and provide an upgrade path to Duo for consideration.
Dark Web Monitoring
The cybersecurity program includes continuous monitoring of company domains and designated
executive accounts to detect any exposed credentials. When verified findings are identified, the
response process includes initiating password resets and conducting access reviews to help ensure
security. A concise summary notification is provided to communicate these actions and findings.
Cybersecurity Awareness Training
OmniWATCH Pro includes a Cybersecurity Awareness Program designed to reduce human-risk
through consistent training, measurable testing, and clear reporting. The program provides new-hire
baseline training to establish core expectations, plus access to a customizable training library that can
be aligned to your policies, roles, and real-world threats (phishing, password hygiene, data handling,
and safe mobile use). Emerge runs proactive phishing campaigns on a regular cadence and provides
reporting on engagement, trends, and improvement opportunities. We also manage an end-user risk
score to help identify where additional coaching is needed and to validate progress over time. When
users receive a suspicious message, they can submit it directly to Emerge for investigation so we can
confirm legitimacy, advise next steps, and respond quickly if a broader campaign is detected.
Incident Response Coordination (Emerge CSEP)
Emerge’s Customer Security Event Plan (CSEP) is our structured, insurance-aware incident
coordination framework that defines how we respond when a suspected or confirmed security event
occurs. CSEP establishes clear roles, escalation paths, evidence-preservation steps, and an
executive communication cadence so decisions are made quickly and documented cleanly. When
activated, Emerge coordinates containment and recovery activities across the affected systems,

partners with any required third parties (e.g., forensic providers, legal counsel, and cyber insurance
stakeholders as directed), and delivers an after-action summary with prioritized hardening
recommendations. The goal is to reduce downtime, limit business impact, and ensure response
actions align with the expectations commonly required during cyber insurance claims and postincident reviews.
*The Emerge CSEP is available for review upon request.
