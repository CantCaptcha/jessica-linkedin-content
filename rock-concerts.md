# Rock Concerts I've Attended

A chronological record of live shows and the memories attached to them.

---

## The Beach Boys

**Date:** August 29, 1987  
**Venue:** Colorado State Fair  
**Notes:** My first real concert

---

## Whitesnake & Kiss

**Date:** May 5, 1990  
**Venue:** Red Rocks Amphitheatre  
**Notes:** The first concert I attended without my parents. With my best friend Bill Foy and Robin Rash.

---

## Janet Jackson (Rhythm Nation 1814 Tour)

**Date:** June 16, 1990  
**Venue:** McNichols Arena  
**Notes:** Went with my sister and Jill Downey (her best friend). It was my present to her for her birthday.

---

## David Bowie (Nine Inch Nails opened)

**Date:** October 16, 1995  
**Venue:** McNichols Arena  
**Notes:** Nine Inch Nails opened for him.

---

## AC/DC (Thunderstruck Tour)

**Date:** January 23, 1991  
**Venue:** McNichols Arena  
**Notes:** We piled way too many people into Ted Cassidy's sedan. I rode in the back seat with Tom Welch and two girls who hitched a ride.

---

## Nine Inch Nails

**Date:** February 8, 1992  
**Venue:** McNichols Arena  
**Notes:** Went with Chris Sjervan from high school. He had a spare ticket. I was in Greeley at the time, he was going to college in Fort Collins. We met at McNichols and attended together. I had never heard NIN before that night. Hooked from the first second Reznor hit the stage. Amazing show on so many levels.

---

## Fleetwood Mac

**Date:** April 1, 2015  
**Venue:** Pepsi Center (now Ball Arena), Denver, CO  
**Notes:** The last time the core members played together in Denver
