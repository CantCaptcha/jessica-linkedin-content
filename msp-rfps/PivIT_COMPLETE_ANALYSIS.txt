- **Local presence** - Charlotte, NC ✅
- **24-month contract term** - Reasonable ✅
- **Awards** - CRN MSP 500, Perfect Google Rating ✅

**Weaknesses:**
- **Business hours NOT specified** ❌ (cannot verify 7AM-5:30PM requirement)
- **NO SLAs provided** ❌ (no response times, resolution times, escalation paths)
- **50% deposit required** for new customers ⚠️ ($2,500 upfront if first month + onboarding)
- **Onsite costs** - $150/hour or $1,200/day (unclear if travel included)
- **Limitation of liability** - Standard but restrictive
- **No explicit CMMC Level 1 certification** (compliance support mentioned)

**Recommendations:**
1. **Clarify business hours coverage** - Confirm 7AM-5:30PM ET requirement
2. **Request SLAs** - Define response times, resolution times, and escalation paths
3. **Clarify onsite costs** - Confirm if travel time included in $150/hour rate
4. **Negotiate deposit** - Waive or reduce 50% deposit requirement
5. **Validate 24/7 SOC coverage** - Confirm 24/7 applies to helpdesk or just EDR monitoring

**Risk Profile:**
- **Medium:** Strong technology and references, but missing SLAs and coverage hours are critical gaps
- **Low:** Competitive pricing, local presence, 24-month contract term

**Overall Assessment:**
PivIT Strategy offers competitive pricing ($165.50/user), 24/7 SOC monitoring, and comprehensive service scope with multi-platform cloud support. The 24-month contract term is reasonable, and the company has strong references and local presence (Charlotte, NC). However, **critical gaps remain**: business hours coverage is not specified, and NO SLAs are provided. The 50% deposit requirement for new customers is also a concern. These gaps prevent full evaluation against RFP requirements and create uncertainty about service commitments. PivIT drops to #4 behind Matrix and ties with TSAChoice.
