## Executive Summary

After analyzing all 6 MSP proposals for Millar Industries, **EmergeITS** emerges as the strongest overall candidate with an **85% score**, followed closely by **Matrix Computer Consulting** at **82%**.

**Key Findings:**
- **EmergeITS** offers the most comprehensive technology stack, strongest DIB experience, and mature support model
- **Matrix** provides excellent pricing ($150-185/user), 90-day stabilization plan, and clear CMMC collaboration
- **Calyra** has competitive pricing ($185/user) and CMMC Level 2 certification, but 5-year contract term and missing coverage hours documentation
- **PivIT** offers 24/7 SOC monitoring and 30-day implementation with competitive pricing ($165.50/user), BUT missing SLAs and coverage hours are critical gaps
- **TSAChoice** has 40+ years experience and local presence but long 36-month contract requirement
- **Midshore** is SDVOSB with CMMC Level 2 success but slower response times and hybrid pricing complexity

**Critical Recommendation:** Schedule deep-dive discovery calls with top 3 (EmergeITS, Matrix, Calyra) to validate pricing assumptions and refine rankings.
