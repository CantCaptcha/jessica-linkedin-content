CMMC Alignment & Collaboration Approach
Overview
Millar Industries has engaged Defense Security Group (DSG) as its cybersecurity and
CMMC compliance partner. PivIT Strategy is purpose-built to work in direct alignment with
DSG, ensuring that all enterprise IT systems, users, and infrastructure fully support CMMC
Level 1 requirements and integrate cleanly with the Level 2 enclave environment.
Our role is to ensure that technology, operations, and security controls are executed
consistently and correctly, enabling DSG to successfully guide Millar through CMMC
requirements.
Collaboration with Defense Security Group (DSG)
PivIT Strategy will act as the operational arm of IT and security, working closely with DSG to:
• Implement and maintain controls required for CMMC Level 1
• Support integration points with the Level 2 enclave
• Execute remediation items identiﬁed by DSG
• Provide visibility, reporting, and validation of controls
• Ensure day-to-day IT operations align with compliance objectives
Our Role in Supporting CMMC Level 1
We operationalize the technical controls required for Level 1, including:
• Access Control & Identity
• MFA enforcement across Microsoft 365 and endpoints
• Secure user provisioning and deprovisioning
• Least privilege access enforcement/management
Endpoint Security
• Deployment and management of Endpoint Detection Response (SentinelOne)
• Device hardening and patching
• Continuous monitoring and response (SOC-backed)
Email & Data Protection
• Proofpoint Advanced email security solution (Business Email Compromise
Protection)
• Secure conﬁguration of Microsoft 365
• Data protection and backup enforcement
