   - "Metrics vs Art" - explore measurement challenges and their art/science tension
   - "Culture Code" - discuss how to maintain values while growing fast
   - "Scale Stories" - share examples of companies who scaled well (or failed) based on culture fit

## What Works Well

- **Morning posts** get 2-3x higher engagement
- **Consistent themes** build recognition as a thought leader
- **3-5 hashtags** max per post (#Leadership, #Operations, #COO, #BusinessStrategy)
- **Reply within 24 hours** shows responsiveness
- **Mix of content types**: Some posts are long-form thought leadership, others are quick insights or metrics

## What to Avoid

- **Posting daily** - spreads content thin
- **Weekend-only posts** - LinkedIn analytics show lower engagement weekends
- **Pure promotional posts** - constant job announcements don't build thought leadership brand
- **Reacting to every comment** - quality over quantity
- **Inconsistent voice** - maintain your authentic tone across all posts
