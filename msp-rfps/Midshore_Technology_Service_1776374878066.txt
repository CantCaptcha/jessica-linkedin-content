Incident Management: All requests generate a ticket, are prioritized by business
impact/urgency, and tracked to closure with end-user confirmation where feasible.

after-hours issues are handled via on-call escalation and after-hours technical
answering service.
Problem Management: Recurring issues are identified via ticket trend review and
addressed with root-cause analysis and corrective actions. Known
issues/workarounds are documented.
Change Management: Changes to production systems follow an approval
workflow, maintenance window coordination, implementation plan, and rollback
plan for higher-risk changes.

MTS fully understands the responsibilities and shared-control boundaries of an MSP/MSSP
in an Organization Seeking Certification (OSC)’s journey to CMMC Level 2 compliance—
including secure administration practices, access control, configuration management,
logging/monitoring, incident response alignment, and the evidence needed to demonstrate
ongoing effectiveness of controls. We have supported Defense Industrial Base (DIB) clients
for more than four years and have recently supported an OSC through a CMMC Level 2

MTS can be as involved in the assessment process as needed to help ensure a successful
certification outcome—ranging from readiness consulting and remediation planning to
implementation support, documentation/evidence preparation, and coordination with
internal stakeholders and third-party vendors. Upon request and under NDA, we can also
use a Service Responsibility Matrix (SRM) approach to clearly define which practices and
artifacts are owned by Millar, MTS, and any other service providers, so responsibilities are
unambiguous during assessment and in ongoing operations.
