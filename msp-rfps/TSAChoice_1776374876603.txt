4. Technology Stack & Tooling
TSAChoice leverages industry-leading technology platforms to deliver comprehensive IT
management and security services. Our technology stack is carefully selected to provide maximum
protection, reliability, and value.

Security Solutions
Advanced Email Threat Protection
Multi-layered email filtering that permits legitimate email while automatically blocking phishing
attempts, impersonation attacks, malware, ransomware, and spam messages. This solution
protects against sophisticated social engineering attacks targeting your employees.
Email Encryption
Secure messaging solution for confidential communications. When required by regulations such as
HIPAA or when handling sensitive business information, emails are automatically encrypted to
ensure data protection in transit.
SOC/SIEM Services (Security Operations Center)
24/7 U.S.-based Security Operations Center providing continuous monitoring of your IT
environment. This service includes threat hunting, log analysis, incident investigation, and
immediate notification of potential security breaches. Compliance reporting and audit support
included.
MDR - Managed Detection and Response
Advanced threat detection service providing continuous monitoring of your network and
endpoints. Using behavioral analytics and threat intelligence, MDR identifies malware,
ransomware, and intrusions in real-time. Includes rapid incident response capabilities for
containment, eradication, and recovery from security incidents.
Dark Web Monitoring
Proactive monitoring service that scans the dark web to identify compromised or stolen employee
and customer credentials. Early detection enables rapid response to credential theft, preventing
unauthorized access to your systems.
Multifactor Authentication (MFA)
Enterprise-grade MFA system that provides an additional security layer beyond passwords. Even if
passwords are compromised, MFA prevents unauthorized access by requiring a second form of
authentication (mobile app, SMS, or hardware token).

Infrastructure Management Tools
Next-Generation Firewall (Hardware as a Service)
Enterprise-class firewall providing comprehensive security including intrusion prevention, content
filtering, application control, and VPN capabilities. Includes hardware, software licensing, and

Remote Connectivity – VPN with Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA)
Secure remote access solution integrated with Zero Trust security principles. Access is granted

Business Continuity Solution (Hardware as a Service)
Datto-based business continuity platform ensuring operations continue uninterrupted during

Azure Services Integration

cloud resources.
Microsoft 365 Licensing & Management

SaaS Protection for Microsoft 365 and Other Platforms
